Perfect Blenheim Location in Marlborough Wine Country

Havino is ideally positioned in Blenheim, offering guests the perfect base for exploring Marlborough's world-renowned wine region. Our luxury accommodation sits within a working vineyard, providing an authentic wine country experience.

Located just minutes from Blenheim Airport, our self-contained villa offers convenient access while maintaining the tranquil privacy of vineyard surroundings. The town center of Blenheim is easily accessible for dining, shopping, and local attractions.

Wine enthusiasts will love our proximity to award-winning wineries and cellar doors. Spend your days touring prestigious vineyards, tasting world-class Sauvignon Blanc and Pinot Noir, then return to your elegant retreat surrounded by endless rows of vines under the expansive Marlborough sky.
